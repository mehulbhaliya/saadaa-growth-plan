New finding · round 2 · N5

The collection page has no navigation — only scroll

Issue: Pure Linen renders as 6 stacked horizontal carousels (Collared Shirt Top, Halter Neck Top, Full Sleeve Shirts, Notch Collar Shirt, Half Sleeve Shirt, Shirts…), each with its own "VIEW ALL". There is no filter, no sort, no price/size facet, no result count, and no Women|Men toggle at the top. To compare two linen shirts a shopper must scroll past everything and open multiple PDPs.

Why it's distinct from F7: F7 was about where the certification sits. This is about information architecture — the page offers no path to narrow intent, which is the single biggest driver of PLP→PDP→ATC on apparel.

Hypothesis: replacing the carousel stack with a filterable/sortable unified grid + sticky facet bar + quick-add increases products-viewed-per-session and PLP→PDP rate → ↑ add-to-cart from collection. Metric: PLP→PDP CTR, ATC-from-PLP, scroll-depth-to-PDP. Tooling: VWO/Optimizely split on the collection template.
